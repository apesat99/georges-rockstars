Help fund children's music therapy in hospitals!

How to help.

There are so many fun ways to help fund music therapy for children in hospital and the more funding we receive the more hospitals we can offer music therapy to and more children will benefit from music therapy while going through tough and stressful times in hospital.

From bake sales, sponsored walks / runs / swims to big challenges and live music events, there are so many ways to support George's Rockstars!

Check out our events page for our upcoming events or get in touch if you have a fundraiser you would like to do for us!

Donations

Thank you so much for considering George's Rockstars for donating your hard earned money to! We hugely appreciate every single donation we receive and unlike large charities, we have minimal costs to cover so you donations go directly to funding children's music therapy!

We are currently in the process of setting up with a new fundraising platform to make donation even easier. For the moment, we are still using our initial Go Fund Me campaign that we set up when we started the charity which you can donate through.

Merchandise

We have a range of George's Rockstars merchandise, from t-shirts and hoodies to lanyards and more! If you would like to purchase merchandise or have some to sell at your event please email us at info@georgesrockstars.com

Company Sponsors

Sponsorships from companies are a huge help to funding our charity! If your company would like to sponsor George's Rockstars, please get in touch! We would love to have you on board!!
